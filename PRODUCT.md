# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and business owners looking for Japanese translation/interpretation services or bilingual technical support in the HVAC/refrigeration industry. They value professionalism, clarity, trust, and accuracy.

## Product Purpose
A clean, multilingual (VI/EN/JP) personal portfolio for Tran Thanh Truc. It showcases her professional profile, Japanese language proficiency (linguistic studies at Hoa Sen University & study abroad experience), and her domain knowledge in refrigeration/HVAC. It acts as a premium digital resume that makes a memorable first impression.

## Brand Personality
Respectful, modern, and minimalist with a clean Japanese-inspired aesthetic. It combines the structured discipline of technical translation with the organic elegance of Japanese art. It is balanced, precise, and professional.

## Anti-references
- SaaS-style generic interfaces with bright purple/pink gradients and glassmorphism.
- Saturated warm cream or sand backgrounds ("beige AI monoculture").
- Over-animated entrance transitions, card grids with side-stripe border accents, and arbitrary section eyebrows (e.g., "01 · ABOUT").
- Messy typography that doesn't adapt well to Japanese characters (kanji/kana).

## Design Principles
- **Ma (Spacial Balance)**: Embrace purposeful whitespace (negative space) to focus the reader's attention. Avoid clutter.
- **Sumi & Aizome Palette**: Use true off-white or cool, light slate neutrals for light mode, and deep indigo (traditional Aizome) or charcoal for dark mode. High contrast and extreme legibility.
- **Typographic Craft**: Font stacks and sizing that handle Latin characters (Vietnamese, English) and East Asian characters (Japanese) with equal grace, ensuring balanced vertical rhythm and line-heights.
- **Respectful Interactions**: Micro-animations should be extremely subtle and gentle—mirroring polite, professional Japanese etiquette.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast compliance (minimum 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Proper ARIA attributes for the language switcher and dark mode toggle.
- Support for system preference `prefers-reduced-motion` and `prefers-color-scheme`.
